Welcome to omc3’s documentation!¶

omc3 is a library for beam optics measurements and corrections in particle accelerators used by the OMC team at CERN.

It consists of various methods for frequency analysis of circular particle accelerators turn-by-turn data, as well as beam optics properties computation and correction algorithms. In addition, it consists of several easy-to-use entrypoint scripts for data analysis, results plotting and MAD-X wrapping.
